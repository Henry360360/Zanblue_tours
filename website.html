FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to common questions about Zanzibar tours

What are the best tours in Zanzibar?
The most popular tours in Zanzibar include Stone Town walking tours, Prison Island visits, spice farm tours, Safari Blue trips, and dolphin watching in Kizimkazi. The best tour for you depends on your interests - whether you prefer cultural experiences, nature, water activities, or relaxation.
Do I need a Covid-19 test for Zanzibar tours?
Covid-19 requirements change frequently. Currently, Zanzibar does not require vaccination proof or testing for entry, but we recommend checking the latest travel guidelines from official sources before your trip. We stay updated on all requirements and will inform you of any necessary preparations.
Can I cancel my day tour?
Yes, you can cancel your tour. Our cancellation policy allows for full refunds if cancelled at least 48 hours before the tour date. Cancellations within 24-48 hours receive a 50% refund, while cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable. Some special tours may have different policies which will be communicated at the time of booking.
How do I make payment?
We accept multiple payment methods including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), bank transfers, and mobile money. For tour bookings, we require a 30% deposit to secure your reservation, with the balance payable upon arrival or before the tour begins depending on the package.
What's included in your tours?
Our tours typically include transportation, professional guide services, entrance fees to attractions, and sometimes meals as specified in the tour description. Water and refreshments are usually provided. Specific inclusions vary by tour, so please check the detailed description of each tour for exact information.
